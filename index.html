Origin

A group of travellers took off on a spaceship set for a new home.

During the trip, a mysterious issue caused the ship to make a crash landing on the seaside cliffs of a tropical world.

When they got out, they were please with their surroundings and met a new friend. He fixes them with a drink and feeling inspired they built a bar out of the broken spaceship.

Overview

An accidental oasis. The premier metaverse destination.

Minting a Bayside NFT will give you access to the broken space-ship bar - affectionately called the whale bar. That's your entry point into the world of Bayside.

Your Bayside NFT will unlock ownership to the town of Bayside as the story unfolds and discoveries are made together.
