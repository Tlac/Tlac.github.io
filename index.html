Origins.

Bayside is an NFT project which follows the story of a group of travellers that took off on a spaceship set for a new home.

During the trip, a mysterious issue caused the ship to crash land on the seaside cliffs of an unknown world.

When they got out, they were pleased with their surroundings and met a new friend. He made them a drink and with some fresh inspiration they built a bar out of the broken spaceship.

Owning a Bayside NFT will be your access point into this new world - as we deliver the content and narratives through seasons.
