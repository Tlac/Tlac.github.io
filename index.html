Origin

Bayside is an NFT project which follows the story of a group of travellers taking off on a spaceship set for a new home.

During the trip, a mysterious issue caused the ship to make a crash landing on the seaside cliffs of an unknown world.

When they got out, they were pleased with their surroundings and amet a new friend. He fixes them with a drink and feeling inspired they built a bar out of the broken spaceship.

OWNING AN NFT WILL BE YOUR ACCESS INTO THE WORLD OF BAYSIDE - AS WE DELIVER OUR CONTENT NARRATIVES THROUGH SEASONS...
